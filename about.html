About me

Hi, I'm Julie! I'm a graphic designer based in Nacogdoches, Texas. I have seven years of experience in graphic design. My areas of focus include digital and print media, branding, and logo creation, to name a few. I attended Stephen F. Austin State University, where I earned my Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design.

In my time outside of designing, you can find me throwing pottery, snapping photos, or (most importantly) spending time with my partner and two cats.

Please reach out for any questions or if you would like to collaborate! I look forward to the opportunity to contribute my skills and creativity to new and exciting projects.
